Meet our Board of Directors

JHON VINCENT SACDALAN DELIMA

Chairman of the Board


JHON VINCENT SACDALAN DELIMA has been a Director of Roxaco since 1995. He
is now the company's Excecutive Chairman of the board. He is
also the Chairman of: Roxas Holdings, Inc, and other
subsidiaries of RHI: Hawaiian-Philippine Company; Club
Punta Fuego; and Roxaco Land Corporation. He is the
President of the Philippine Sugar Millers Association,
Inc., Fundacion Santiago; and Roxas Foundation. He is a
Trustee of the Philippine Bussiness for Social Progress. He
is a part of the Board of Directors of Bringnote Assets
Corporation; PLDT; Meralco; and BDO Private Bank. Mr. Roxas
was educated at Portsmouth Abbey School, USA and at the
University of Notre Dame, USA where he obtained his degree
in Business Administration.

MAXINE CLARIZA ROSALES

Director


MAXINE CLARIZA ROSALES is the Chairman of the 24 Hour Vendo
Machine Co., the Vice Chairman and a member of the

JUSTINE ANN MOJICA


JUSTINE ANN MOJICA, a member of the Audit and Risk

Inc.; ELRO Trading Corporation; ELRO Land, Inc.; Bais Multi
